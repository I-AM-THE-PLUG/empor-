About Empower Africa Network

Learn about our mission, vision, and the journey of empowering communities across Africa

Who We Are

Empower Africa Network (EAN) is a Pan-African non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals and communities across Africa through skills-based training, entrepreneurial support, education, and community development initiatives.

Founded with a vision of sustainable development, we work directly with underserved communities to provide practical solutions that address the root causes of poverty and unemployment.

Our Mission

Empower individuals and communities in Africa with skills, resources, and support to drive economic growth and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

A prosperous Africa where individuals and communities thrive through skills, entrepreneurship, and empowerment.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

EAN was founded in Tamale, Ghana with a pilot skills training program for 50 young people

2020

Expansion

Expanded to 3 additional communities and launched our entrepreneur support program

2022

National Recognition

Recognized by the Ghanaian government for outstanding community development work

2023

Regional Growth

Extended programs to neighboring West African countries and established regional partnerships

Our Core Values

Community-Centered

We put communities at the heart of everything we do, listening to their needs and co-creating solutions

Sustainable Impact

We focus on long-term, measurable outcomes that create lasting change in people's lives

Inclusive Growth

We ensure our programs reach the most vulnerable and marginalized populations

Innovation

We embrace creative approaches and leverage technology to maximize our impact
